# Small Team AI Fluency GTM Plan

## Positioning

Project Sapiens helps small teams build practical AI fluency through role-based lessons, guided labs, and measurable workflow projects.

The core promise:

> Train your team to use AI safely, practically, and measurably in the work they already do.

This is not positioned as a generic AI course library. It is a team enablement sprint that turns AI curiosity into repeatable workflows, shared standards, and visible progress.

## Ideal Customer Profile

Start with teams of 5-25 people where the buyer feels AI pressure but does not have an internal AI enablement function.

Best early segments:

- Small professional services firms
- Marketing and content teams
- Customer support or operations teams
- Nonprofit and education admin teams
- Founder-led companies that need practical AI adoption
- Local business networks, chambers, accelerators, and workforce programs

Avoid in the first 30 days:

- Large enterprise procurement
- Deep compliance-heavy verticals unless the buyer already trusts Tony
- Fully custom academies
- Generic consumer self-serve growth

## Offer

### Small Team AI Fluency Sprint

A 4-week guided program for 5-25 employees.

Includes:

- Team baseline assessment
- Practical AI fundamentals
- Role-specific practice labs
- Shared prompt/workflow library
- Three customized workflow labs
- Final AI workflow playbook
- Manager summary report
- Optional live kickoff and closing workshop

Outcomes:

- Employees know when and how to use AI
- The team has approved examples for real workflows
- Managers can see completion and engagement
- The company leaves with reusable prompt and workflow assets

## Customization Model

Customization should be a core part of the offer, but bounded.

### Level 1: Light Customization

Fastest to sell and deliver.

Includes:

- Cohort name
- Company/team goals
- Selected learning path
- Role-specific practice prompts
- Industry-flavored examples
- Final project tied to the buyer's workflow

Use this for most pilots.

### Level 2: Workflow Packs

The likely sweet spot for repeatable revenue.

Examples:

- AI for Sales Teams
- AI for Marketing Teams
- AI for Operations
- AI for Customer Support
- AI for Managers
- AI for Nonprofit Teams

Each pack should include:

- 4-6 lessons
- 3-5 labs
- prompt templates
- workflow checklist
- final project rubric
- manager discussion guide

### Level 3: Private Company Academy

Higher-ticket and slower to deliver.

Includes:

- Branded workspace
- Private cohort
- company-approved use cases
- internal AI policies or guidelines
- custom lessons
- manager dashboard
- certificates
- optional workshops

Do not lead with this in the first 30 days. Offer it only when a buyer asks for deeper customization.

## Product Packaging

### Pilot

Target price: $2,500-$7,500.

Best for 5-25 employees and one department.

Deliverables:

- 4-week sprint
- three customized workflow labs
- final workflow playbook
- completion report

### Team

Target price: $500-$1,500/month plus setup.

Best for teams that want ongoing training and prompt/workflow maintenance.

Deliverables:

- recurring access
- monthly workflow challenge
- manager report
- updated prompt packs

### Academy

Target price: custom.

Best for organizations that want private branded training and multiple cohorts.

Deliverables:

- custom content
- private cohorts
- deeper reporting
- optional live facilitation

## 30-Day Execution Plan

### Week 1: Sharpen The Offer

- Rewrite landing copy around small-team AI fluency.
- Create a one-page sales PDF or web page.
- Define the pilot scope, price range, and customization boundaries.
- Create one sample workflow pack: AI for Small Teams.
- Fix high-trust product issues: TypeScript typecheck failures, obvious route/prop contract issues, and pricing copy mismatch.

### Week 2: Build The Demo Path

- Create a clean demo cohort flow using existing paths, labs, prompts, and admin features.
- Add sample team roles: manager, marketer, operator, support rep.
- Create three polished workflow labs:
  - meeting notes to actions
  - customer feedback analysis
  - first-draft business communication
- Prepare a manager summary report mock.
- Prepare a final AI workflow playbook template.

### Week 3: Sell Pilots

- Build a target list of 50 warm or reachable buyers.
- Send personalized outreach focused on team AI adoption pain.
- Offer 3 pilot slots.
- Run discovery calls around actual workflows, not generic AI interest.
- Close at least one paid pilot or one highly committed design partner.

### Week 4: Deliver And Learn

- Run the first pilot or simulated pilot with a friendly team.
- Collect baseline and completion data.
- Document before/after workflows.
- Capture objections and friction.
- Convert what worked into a repeatable workflow pack.

## Sales Message

Short version:

> Your team is probably already using AI, but unevenly. Project Sapiens gives them a practical, shared way to use AI for real work: writing, analysis, planning, decisions, and workflow improvement.

Longer version:

> Project Sapiens is a 4-week AI fluency sprint for small teams. We adapt our curriculum to your team's roles and workflows, then guide employees through practical labs that produce reusable prompts, workflow templates, and a final team AI playbook. The goal is not AI hype. The goal is confident, responsible, repeatable use.

## Discovery Questions

Use these on buyer calls:

- Where is your team already using AI informally?
- What work is repetitive, writing-heavy, research-heavy, or analysis-heavy?
- What concerns do you have about quality, privacy, accuracy, or consistency?
- Which roles would benefit first?
- What would make this training feel useful within two weeks?
- What would a successful final project look like?
- Who needs to see progress or outcomes?

## Success Metrics

Commercial:

- 3 pilot conversations in 30 days
- 1 paid pilot closed
- $2,500+ pilot revenue
- 1 repeatable workflow pack validated

Product:

- typecheck passes
- demo path can be completed by a new user
- manager report is understandable without explanation

- top 5 objections
- completion rate across pilot
- before/after confidence shift
